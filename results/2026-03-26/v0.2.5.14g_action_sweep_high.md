# v0.2.5.14g — 12-Policy Action Scale Sweep (0.5--1.5)

**Date:** 2026-03-26
**SLURM Job:** 7612686 (~111 min)
**Notebook:** `experiments/2026-03-26/MVP_v0.2.5.14g_action_sweep_high.ipynb`
**Builds on:** v0.2.5.14e (12 policies, rho=0.622 at scale=0.5), v0.2.5.14d (6 policies, rho=0.886 at scale=0.4)

## Goal

Test whether the monotonically increasing Spearman rho trend observed at lower scales (0.03--0.5) continues into the higher range, or collapses at stronger guidance.

## Setup

- **12 target policies** spanning oracle SR 0%--90%
- **Sweep:** action_scale in [0.5, 0.6, 0.7, 0.8, 0.9, 1.0, 1.25, 1.5]
- **Diffuser:** TemporalUnet (dim=32, mults=(1,4,8)), 256 steps, chunk_size=4, action_weight=5.0
- **Scorer:** RobomimicDiffusionScorer, score_timestep=5, sigmoid_sharpness=150.0
- **50 synthetic trajectories** per policy, T=60, gamma=1.0
- **Total:** 96 guided runs (12 policies x 8 scales), ~70s each

## Key Findings

1. **Guidance collapses at high action_scale.** The monotonically increasing rho trend from v0.2.5.14e does NOT continue -- it reverses sharply above 0.5.

2. **Scale >= 1.0 kills all synthetic trajectories.** At action_scale 1.0, 1.25, and 1.5, every policy produces 0% synthetic success rate, making rho_hard undefined (NaN). Scale 0.9 is nearly collapsed (only 2/12 policies have any non-zero SR, both at 4%).

3. **RMSE worsens monotonically with scale.** logRMSE_hard saturates at 12.47 for scale >= 1.0 (all hard rewards are 0).

4. **Sigmoid scoring is more robust** at moderate scales (rho_sigmoid=0.622 vs rho_hard=0.545 at scale=0.5), but also collapses by scale 0.8.

5. **Combined picture with v0.2.5.14e:** The optimal action_scale for 12 policies peaks in the 0.3--0.5 range, with clear collapse above 0.5. The scale=0.4 sweet spot from v0.2.5.14d (6 policies, rho=0.886) remains the best known operating point.

## Implications

The action_scale hyperparameter has a well-defined optimum. Future work should fine-tune in the 0.3--0.5 range rather than pushing higher. The collapse mechanism appears to be guidance overpowering the diffusion prior, destroying trajectory coherence.
